ORANGE HOTEL SUPERIOR APARTMENT

The Orange Colom Apartments have 6 superior apartments, 2 of them smaller but with larger terrace and another 4 larger ones with 2 smaller terraces.

The apartments have two single beds, except for 305, which has a double bed, a complete bathroom with a shower, a living room with sofa bed, a dining table, satellite TV and a kitchen area with a ceramic hob, microwave and fridge.

These apartments also have terrace with stunning views over the Mediterranean sea, table and chairs. Two of them have hammocks.
